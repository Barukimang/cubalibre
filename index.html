How It Works

Investment without
the middlemen.

Traditional platforms take cuts, offer no transparency, and give you zero protection. CubaLibre is different — your money stays in a Safe you control until an AI agent verifies each milestone.

1

Browse Opportunities

Discover Cuban businesses seeking capital — restaurants, shops, artisans. Each opportunity has clear milestones and a transparent funding goal.

2

Commit to Safe Escrow

Your funds go into a 2-of-2 Safe multisig — you never fully lose control. The AI agent (Satoshi Nakaboto) is the co-signer. No platform can touch your money.

3

AI Verifies & Releases

Business completes milestones, submits evidence. Satoshi verifies, then co-signs the Safe transaction to release funds. You stay informed at every step.
